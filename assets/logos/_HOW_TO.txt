HOW TO ADD LOGOS
────────────────
1. Download the logo image (PNG or JPG) from the company/school website
2. Drop it into THIS folder  (assets/logos/)
3. Open content.js and update the "logo" field in the right section
   e.g.  logo: "assets/logos/sutd_logo.png"

TIPS
────
• PNG with transparent background looks best
• Square images work best (~200×200px or larger)
• If no logo is provided, the site shows initials automatically

SUGGESTED FILES TO ADD
──────────────────────
• sutd_logo.png      — SUTD logo (download from sutd.edu.sg)
• workato_logo.png   — Workato logo (download from workato.com/press)
• [company]_logo.png — Any other company or school logos
