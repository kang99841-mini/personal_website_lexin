HOW TO ADD YOUR PROFILE PHOTO
──────────────────────────────
1. Get a good headshot or professional photo of yourself
2. Rename the file to:  profile.jpg  (or profile.png)
3. Drop it into THIS folder  (assets/photos/)
4. The site will automatically use it

TIPS
────
• Square photos work best (1:1 ratio)
• At least 400×400px
• Good lighting, neutral background looks most professional
• If no photo is added, your initials "LX" show instead — that's fine too!

If you want to use a different filename, open content.js and update:
    photo: "assets/photos/YOUR_FILENAME_HERE.jpg"
