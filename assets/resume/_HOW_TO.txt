HOW TO ADD YOUR RESUME
──────────────────────
1. Export your resume as a PDF
2. Name the file:  Lexin_Resume.pdf
3. Drop it into THIS folder  (assets/resume/)
4. The download link on your site will work automatically

If you want to use a different filename, open content.js and update:
    resume_file: "assets/resume/YOUR_FILENAME_HERE.pdf"
