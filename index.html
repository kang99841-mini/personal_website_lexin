About

I'm a Data Engineer on ByteDance's Data Team, embedded in the Live Streaming vertical. My primary responsibility is owning the live stream costing pipelines — systems that calculate costs across the entire live streaming infrastructure, surface price mismatches, and support root-cause diagnosis at scale.

Day-to-day this means writing performant Hive SQL over petabyte tables, tuning Spark jobs for skew and shuffle efficiency, and understanding the in-house data warehouse deeply enough to make good architectural decisions — not just get things working.

I care about the why behind every design choice and believe that cost attribution done right is one of the highest-leverage things an engineering team can invest in.
